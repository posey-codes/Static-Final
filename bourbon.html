Bourbon


Bourbon is an American made Whiskey and contrary to popular belief, does not have to be distilled only in Kentucky. It does however, require that the mash be made up from at least 51% corn to be considered a "bourbon". There also is a type of bourbon labeled Kentucky Straight Bourbon, this bourbon would have been made in Kentucky. Some popular brands of Bourbon includes Blantons, Eagle Rare, and Buffalo Trace. These are highly sought after bottles that aren't always the easiest to come by.

There are many different ways to enjoy bourbon. Some people will mix into a soft drink such as coke, however this can definitely be frowned upon if you're trying a nicer bourbon as you miss out on a lot of the taste. Some can be enjoyed warm straight into a glass or poured over some ice.

One of the most common ways is pouring them straight into a cold glass
